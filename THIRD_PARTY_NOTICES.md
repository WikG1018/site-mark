# Third-party notices

SiteMark includes open-source dependencies distributed through Flutter pub and
Rust crates.io. Their license metadata is recorded in `pubspec.lock` and
`rust/Cargo.lock`.

## Noto Sans CJK SC

The bundled `rust/assets/fonts/NotoSansSC-Regular.otf` is part of Noto Sans CJK
and is used to render Simplified Chinese and Latin watermark text consistently.

Font License 1.1. The complete license is included at
`rust/assets/fonts/OFL.txt`.
